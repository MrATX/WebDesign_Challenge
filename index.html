Summary: Latitude vs X


The visualizations organized herein detail a simple analysis of geographic characteristics of thousands of cities across the world, compared to the city's latitude.

Analyzing Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed, the results are visualized and organized here to be perused at your leisure. You can access the visualizations from the sidebar on the right side of this page, or from the appropriate links at the top right of the nav bar.
